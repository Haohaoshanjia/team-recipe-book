Vegetarian Cordon Bleu

By: HC

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms (caps cleaned and stems removed)
  • 4 slices of vegetarian ham or smoked tofu
  • 4 slices of Swiss cheese (or plant-based cheese)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs (or flax eggs for vegan option)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place a slice of vegetarian ham and cheese inside each mushroom cap, then cover with another mushroom cap to form a "sandwich."
  3. Prepare three bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs mixed with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Dip each mushroom sandwich first in flour, then in egg, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat olive oil in a skillet and lightly fry each coated mushroom until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake for 15–20 minutes until cheese is melted and mushrooms are tender.
  7. Serve hot with a side salad or roasted vegetables.

Cooking time: 35 minutes

Serves: 4 people
