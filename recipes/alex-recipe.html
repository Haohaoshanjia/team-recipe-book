Beijing Style Zhajiangmian

By: Alexander Shan

4 out of 5
2-4 hours
4-6 people

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • water
  • 2-3 eggs
  • ground pork or beef
  • tianmianjiang
  • vegetable oil
  • cucumber

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Add the eggs to the flour and water to make dough. You want to add a bit of excess flour on top after making it.
  2. Step 2: Make sure the dough absorbs the flour and continuously flatten it make it back into a lump for 3-5 times.
  3. Step 3: Flatten the dough into a thin flap, add flour on both faces, and layer it up into 3-5 layers
  4. Step 4: Cut the layered flap into strips and leave them alone for a later step
  5. Step 5: Add the ground beef and tianmianjiang sauce into a frying pan and add a good amount of vegetable oil
  6. Step 6: Keep on stirring and mixing it while it fries, after 15-30 minutes when the meat is no longer raw, you can put it into a bowl.
  7. Step 7: add the strips of dough into a pot with boiling water, and your noodles should be ready in 15-30 minutes
  8. Step 8: Cut the cucumbers into strips.
  9. Step 9: mix the cucumber strips with noodles and the ground meat with the sauce, and you are done!

Cooking and Prep time: 2-4 hours

Serves: 4-6 people
