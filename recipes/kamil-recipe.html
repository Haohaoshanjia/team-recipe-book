Chicken Biryani

By: Kamil Balsara

5 out of 5
4-5 hours
6 people

Ingredients:

  • 16 pieces of whole chicken
  • 3 cups of rice
  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 9 onions
  • 1 bunch of coriander
  • 1/2 bunch of mint
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons of ginger
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of garlic
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon of green chilli
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 cups of oil
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 pack of biryani spicing

Instructions:

  1. Add lemon to the chicken and wash.
  2. Take 1 big pot and pour 2 cups of oil
  3. Chop 5 onions into the pot and let them fry into a light brown color
  4. Blend the 3 tomatoes and pour into pot
  5. Add 1 cup of yogurt into pot
  6. Put 2 1/2 tablespoon salt into pot
  7. Add biryani spicing into pot and mix
  8. Add all chicken into pot and let it cook for 2 hours
  9. Take a second pot and fill it halfway with water. Then add 1 tablespoon of salt and 1 1/2 tablespoon oil into second pot
  10. Let water boil for 25 minutes
  11. Let rice boil as well for 25 minutes
  12. Pour rice into filter, once done put it aside for now
  13. Now chop 4 onions and 2 potatoes
  14. Take a small pot and pour 1 liter of oil
  15. Let oil boil for 15 mins
  16. Fry onions light brown
  17. Take a plate and place a tissue on it, then pour fried onions on tissue
  18. Repeat same process for potatoes
  19. Chop coriander and mint, then wash them and place into filter
  20. Take the rice pot and place a foil paper in it at the bottom
  21. Pour 3 tablespoon oil into rice pot and pour 1 1/2 cup rice into bottom of pot
  22. Take half of the chicken and place it on the bottom with rice
  23. Now pour half of the onions, coriander, potatoes, and mint as well to complete the bottom layer
  24. Then pour remaining half of all ingredients and complete the second layer
  25. Now take a circular piece of foil paper larger than the pot and cover it at the top
  26. Finally, place the lid onto the pot, and then place it on low heat for 1 hour

Cooking time: 4-5 hours

Serves: 6 people
